About

Claire Mckenna passed away October 12th of 2022. Across the world today we have sadly witnessed many accidental overdoses due to drugs that are laced with fentanyl.This unfortunately happened to our Claire. We hope that she is now at peace and looking over her friends and family. We want claire to be remembered by her infectious smile, kind personality and amazing ability to make music. She was an amazing singer and creator. Claire’s story upsettingly was twisted and made up all throughout articles on the web, creating a false narrative of her passing . Although we attempted to remove many and successfully did so as it went against journalistic guidelines and facts ,all are not gone. We want everyone to know that those articles are deeply false , wrong and wildly insensitive. We have had put in a lot of time and work into deleting them and addresses the sources who made false information up for a story . The family wants people to know the truth and so would claire. We ask you to remember our beautiful claire always and to honor her by continuing to listen to her music.
